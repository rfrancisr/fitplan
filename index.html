Structure

Two phases.
One transformation.

The first four weeks build your base. The second four weeks carve the shape. Each phase demands more than the last.

01
Foundation & hypertrophy
Weeks 1–4
High volume, 60s rest between sets. Your body learns the movements and starts growing. Form over everything.
02
Intensification & definition
Weeks 5–8
Harder variations, supersets, rest drops to 30–45s. This is where the V-shape emerges. Expect soreness to return.

Trainer notes

What actually matters.

The V-shape formula
Wide lats + capped shoulders + narrow waist. Pull-ups are your single best exercise. If you can't do them yet, start with negatives — jump up, lower slowly for 5 seconds. Volume beats intensity in the first four weeks. I've had clients gain a full shirt size in lats in 6 weeks with negatives alone.
30 min vs 1 hour
On shorter days, cut sets to 3 and reduce rest to 45s. Keep every exercise exactly as listed — no skipping. On longer days, add a 3-minute push-up burnout or plank holds as a finisher at the end of every session.
Sleep is the training
Muscle is built during sleep, not during training. 8 hours minimum. Cold shower after training reduces inflammation. 10–15 min sun exposure daily supports testosterone. Cut alcohol completely — it directly suppresses protein synthesis.
Pull-ups without a bar
Inverted rows under a sturdy table are 80% as effective for lat development. The more horizontal your body, the harder the pull. Nearly flat = equivalent to a full pull-up. A tree branch, playground bar, or staircase banister also works perfectly.
Supplements worth using
Creatine monohydrate 5g/day — most studied supplement for strength and muscle volume. Vitamin D3 2000 IU. Magnesium glycinate before bed for sleep quality. Whey protein only if you genuinely can't hit 200g from food.
Phase 2 mindset
Weeks 5–8, expect soreness to return around week 5. That is the signal it's working. Do not back off. Your body knows the movements now — harder variations will trigger growth that phase 1 couldn't. This is where the shape emerges.
