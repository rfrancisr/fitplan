V-shape formula

The V-taper is about wide lats + capped shoulders + a narrow waist. Pull-ups are your single best friend. Do them every back day, and if you can't do them yet, start with negatives (jump up, lower slowly for 5 seconds) and inverted rows under a table.
Actor trick: I had a client gain a full shirt size in his lats in 6 weeks with negatives alone. Volume beats intensity early — get the reps in.

The 30-min vs 1-hr rule

30 minutes: Do the full workout but cut sets to 3 and reduce rest to 45s. Keep the exercises exactly as listed.
1 hour: Add a finisher — 3 min of push-up burnout or plank holds at the end of every session.

Sleep & recovery

Muscle is built during sleep, not during training. 8 hours minimum. Cold shower after training reduces inflammation. Aim for 10–15 min sun exposure daily for natural testosterone production. Alcohol directly suppresses protein synthesis — cut it out completely during these 8 weeks.
